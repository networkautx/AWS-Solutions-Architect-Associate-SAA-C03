# AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C03) Exam Summary

The **AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C03)** certification validates your ability to design secure, resilient, high-performing, and cost-optimized architectures on AWS.

---

## Exam Overview

| Feature                 | Details                               |
| :---------------------- | :------------------------------------ |
| **Exam Code**           | SAA-C03                               |
| **Exam Duration**       | 130 Minutes                           |
| **Number of Questions** | 65                                    |
| **Question Types**      | Multiple Choice and Multiple Response |
| **Passing Score**       | 720 (Scaled Score)                    |
| **Score Range**         | 100–1,000                             |
| **Exam Fee**            | USD $150 + Applicable Taxes           |

---

## Question Breakdown

The exam contains **65 questions**, divided into:

* **50 Scored Questions** – Count toward your final certification score.
* **15 Unscored Questions** – Used by AWS to evaluate future exam questions. These are randomly distributed throughout the exam and are **not identified**.

---

## Language Accommodation

Candidates whose primary language is **not English** may request **30 additional minutes** through the AWS Exam Accommodations process.

This extra time can be especially beneficial for Professional and Specialty certification exams.

---

## Exam Delivery Options

You can take the AWS SAA-C03 exam using either of the following methods:

### Online Proctored Exam

Take the exam remotely from your home or office under live online supervision.

### Testing Center

Take the exam at an authorized **Pearson VUE** testing center.

---

## Online Exam Recommendations

If you choose the online proctored option:

* Join the exam **30 minutes before** your scheduled time.
* Complete the identity verification process early.
* Ensure your room is quiet, clean, and free from unauthorized materials.
* Test your webcam, microphone, and internet connection beforehand.
* Close all unnecessary applications before launching the exam.

---

## Preparation Tips

* Understand AWS architectural concepts instead of memorizing facts.
* Gain hands-on experience with core AWS services.
* Review AWS documentation and whitepapers.
* Practice scenario-based questions regularly.
* Take multiple mock exams to improve confidence and time management.
